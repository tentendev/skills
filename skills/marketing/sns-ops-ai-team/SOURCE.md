## Distilled essence

The transferable method is a **role-based delegation structure** for social-media
operations: six operating roles (Strategy Lead, Researcher, Planner, Editor,
Marketer, Secretary) each handed to AI, with the human shifted from producer to
final approver (拍板). Key generalizable rule: **authorize one role at a time**
rather than building the full team at once; the real payoff is concentrating human
effort on decisions, not just cutting hours.
